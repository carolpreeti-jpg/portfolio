About Me

In 2021, I started
a career led by
visual strategy.

I'm Carol, a creative lead and art director
based in Brazil. I build designs that carry
strategy and meaning.

My work has been part of campaigns
for brands across Brazil, building visual identities,
high-performance social media designs, print,
and AI-powered web development.

I've always loved telling stories, and I found design and audiovisual to be the perfect places to put this passion into practice. For the past five years, I've been working in this field, exploring a wide range of areas: from social media design and visual identities to presentations and print materials.

For me, bridging design and audiovisual is all about building immersive worlds. This constant drive to innovate naturally led me to technology. Today, I leverage AI to develop modern websites and architect custom AI agents, transforming complex tech into striking, intuitive human experiences.

Sticking to just one creative canvas was never enough and driven by a love for narrative and indie culture, I stepped behind the camera in 2025 to make my debut as a film director and screenwriter.

Whether I'm writing a script, laying out an editorial project, or prompting an intelligent agent, I am always seeking the unexpected and the cutting-edge.
